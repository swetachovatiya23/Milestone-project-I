Trifle Game


Instructions for the Game...



1. Start the Quiz by clicking bellow button.

2. You will have diffrent Categorise, select any Categorise and proceed.

3. Once you select Category you will find 10 questions.

4. You have to select options of those Questions.

5. Each correct answer will be rewarded as 1 point.

6. You can always change the Quiz Category by clicking the Next Quiz option.

7. After answering 10 Questions, Final score will be displayed based on your performance.
